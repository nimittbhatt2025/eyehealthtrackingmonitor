4. **Filter further**: Select category if needed
5. **Explore**: Click any condition for details

## 💡 Pro Tips

### For Best AI Results:
- ✅ Be specific: "eyes burn after 2 hours of computer work"
- ✅ Not vague: "eyes hurt"
- ✅ Include timing: "worse at night", "after reading"
- ✅ Mention activities: "during screen time", "in bright light"

## ⚠️ IMPORTANT: When to See a Doctor

The AI chatbot and library will tell you, but ALWAYS see an eye doctor if:

🚨 **Urgent (Go Today):**
- Sudden vision loss
- Sudden severe eye pain
- Seeing flashes of light
- New floaters or spots
- Eye injury
- Double vision
- Severe headache with vision changes

⚠️ **Soon (Schedule Appointment):**
- Persistent blurry vision
- Vision that doesn't clear
- Increasing light sensitivity
- Worsening symptoms
- Symptoms affecting daily life
- Recommended by AI (high urgency)

✓ **Regular Checkups:**
- Annual comprehensive eye exam
- Every 6 months if you have risk factors
- After major vision changes
- To update prescriptions
